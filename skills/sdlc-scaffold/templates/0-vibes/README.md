# 0 — Vibes

The earliest, pre-planning stage. Raw ideas, gut feelings, inspiration, and
loose direction before anything is structured. The **vibe** — where a product
starts before it becomes concrete business tasks.

An item leaves this stage when a vibe is shaped into a PRD (see `prd/`) and
handed off to `../1-business-tasks/`.

## Structure
- `prd/` — Product Requirements Documents: the first structured artifact that
  turns a vibe into a stated product intent.

## What goes here
- Raw ideas & inspiration
- Sketches, moodboards, references
- Early product direction and intent
