Each observation task is `OT-{number}-{TYPE}.md`, where `TYPE` is the severity
folder it sits in, and contains everything needed to understand and act on it.

Record what was observed, not what you think it means. An observation is
evidence; the diagnosis belongs in the planning task that cites it.

Interview the creator until you share an understanding of the signal. **If no
human is reachable — you are a spawned agent, and often will be — do not guess.**
Write the open questions into the task under a heading that says so.
