# 1 — Business tasks source (observation & planning)

Origin of all work. Capture raw business needs, observations, research, and
high-level plans here — the **why** behind everything downstream.

An item leaves this stage when it is understood well enough to be specified in
`../2-specs/`.

## Structure
- `observation/` — signals gathered from the running system, users, and ops,
  triaged by severity into `errors/`, `warnings/`, and `infos/`.
- `planning/` — high-level plans, roadmaps, and priorities shaped from what
  observation surfaces and from business goals.

## What goes here
- Observations & research notes
- Stakeholder input / business goals
- Opportunity write-ups and high-level plans
